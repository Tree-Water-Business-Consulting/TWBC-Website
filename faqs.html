More Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can I have an ad on the screen?

Once your artwork is ready to go and we've received your payment, your ad can be on a screen within 3 days. 

How do I pay for my ads?

You will pay for your first month's ad when you sign up online.  Subsequent monthly payments will be automatically debited from the account you specify. We accept all major credit cards and bank debit.

When does billing start?

Monthly payments will be automatically debited from the account you specify 30 days after your approved artwork is active on one of our screens. We accept all major credit cards and bank debit.

Is my information secure?

YES! We use Zoho to collect and process our data. Your data's privacy and security is our top priority. With proven network security components in place to ensure the highest security for your data, you can rest assured. This includes physical security practices (like 7x24x365 security, video monitoring, biometric access, bullet-resistant walls), network security (encryption, intrusion detection/prevention), people processes and redundancy/business continuity. Please refer the following link to know more about Zoho Security Practices, Policy and Infrastructure: http://www.zoho.com/security.html

What if I want to put my ad on multiple screens?

Yes, you can put your ad on multiple screens.  Just Contact Us.

Can you help improve my design?

We sure can!  Upload what you have and we will do what we can to make it POP!  If you don't have any design, we can create one for you. You can take any design we create for you for use throughout your business.

Do I need to provide you with mobile and tablet designs?

No. We will make sure your ad fits to our screens.

Are fees refundable?

No, fees are not refundable. As long as you give us 5 days notice, you can cancel any upcoming automatic payments.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Email us at info@treewaterbusiness.com! We can cancel your subscription within 2 business days.
